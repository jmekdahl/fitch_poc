CLO Deal Abstract Tool

Overview

Fitch Ratings's CLO Indenture Abstracts provide unprecedented insight into the key features of the CLO indenture and how those features compare to other Fitch-rated CLOs. An Indenture Abstract will be published for every Fitch-rated U.S. CLO 2.0.

In each report, Fitch reviews and describes the following indenture features:

  • Collateral quality tests;
  • Concentration limitations;
  • Coverage tests;
  • Priority of payments;
  • Conditions to reinvestment;
  • Events of default;
  • Manager replacement provisions;
  • Call provisions;
  • Indenture amendments; and
  • Reporting requirements

As investors continue to deal with rapidly growing CLO volume, their ability to dissect and digest the transaction documents presents an increasing challenge. CLO indentures lack uniformity and evolve over time to reflect current changes in the underlying loan market. Fitch's CLO Indenture Abstracts are aimed at easing this burden by highlighting the indenture features that investors have indicated are most critical in their analysis.
